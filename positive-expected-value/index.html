Positive Expected Value

Tristan Wiggill January 15, 2023

One of the best ways to make money long-term in sports betting is to consistently find and place positive expected value (positive EV) bets.

Ok, hold up. What the heck are positive EV bets?

In simple terms, positive EV bets are bets that have a higher probability of winning than what the bookmaker's odds suggest.

To make money, you have to find these pricing abnormalities, while also overcoming the bookmakers commission/margin/vig. This is why finding a bookmaker with the lowest margins is so important.

Successful betting is hard! You need to find every advantage you can and exploit it. That's what we aim to do here on EPL Bets.

You must understand that there are two types of odds. The first are known as the true odds. It is difficult to find true odds as bookmakers purposely offer odds/prices that are lower than the true probabilities. 

The second are the bookmakers odds.  

The primary reason that sports book the world over make money is because the majority of bettors bet on outcomes that have polar opposite i.e. negative expected value.
