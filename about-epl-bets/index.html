About EPL Bets

Tristan Wiggill January 15, 2023

Welcome to EPL Bets!

As a dedicated English Premier League (EPL) fan, I am passionate about researching and analysing EPL teams, players and managers to find interesting and profitable betting opportunities.

My goal is to provide free and valuable information to bettors looking to profit from betting on the EPL. I spend countless hours researching and analysing the latest news, statistics, and trends in the most watched football league in the world.

I study the performance of teams and players, documenting their strengths and weaknesses, to provide valuable insights and predictions that can help you make informed and profitable EPL bets.

I am always looking for positive expected value and the highest odds during my research. This means identifying bets that are priced higher than the probabilities suggest. It is critical that we find an edge over the bookmakers for a better chance to make a profit.

I believe that everyone should have access to the information they need to make informed and profitable EPL bets. That's why I make all of my research available free of charge, so that anyone can benefit from my hard work and expertise.

I make it easy for you to access the information you need, so you can focus on placing your bets and making a profit.
