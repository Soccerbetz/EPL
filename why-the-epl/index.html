Why the EPL?

Tristan Wiggill January 15, 2023

Why do I choose to bet on the English Premier League?

From a purely betting perspective, the EPL is challenging and unpredictable. Several teams can win the league. Any team, no matter the odds or league position, can beat another. 

The EPL betting market is highly efficient. This means the odds offered by the leading sharp sports books are more accurate than the lesser-known competitions. This is not to say that bookies don't get their prices wrong. They do, and this is what we look to exploit. 

One advantage with betting on the EPL is the liquidity it offers bettors. What this translates into are generally higher betting limits/stakes. 

Most of the value in the EPL is found on the player prop markets. Some of the more interesting ones include player goals, player and team cards and corners. Money can also be made on the more traditional markets such as 1x2 (moneyline), the over/unders goal markets and European or Asian handicap markets.

Mathematically, it is more difficult to beat the commission/margin on 3-way markets (1X2) than it is on 2-way markets (overs/unders).
